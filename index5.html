about us

about us
Educational agency Bartstudy Poland and computer graphics training center Bartschool We prepare our students for admission to universities in Poland in areas such as graphic design, 3D computer graphics, computer game design.
We have been working since 2016.

ONLINE COURSES:

graphic design, illustration, animation, 2D,3D computer graphics, mobile application design and others

PROGRAMS:

Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, CorelDraw, 3D MAX, Adobe After Effects, Zbrush, Maya, Unity, Cinema 4D

HOW THE TRAINING IS DONE:

Training takes place only in real time, under the supervision of an experienced teacher and mentor. Regular reports to parents about the work done and the child’s work.
Programs in which classes are held: ZOOM, DISCORD

WHAT MAKES US SET UP:

We prepare for admission to a university in Poland; all our students successfully pass the entrance campaign thanks to preparation and portfolio.
